Project Overview

As an Associate Software Engineer specializing in UI/UX, I worked on the Accu TCM Habit platform, which offers self-guided acupressure treatments. This was one of the first projects I engaged in during my initial months, where my primary responsibilities involved image modification and UI design adjustments for mobile screens.

Initial Tasks and Image Modifications

  • Design Approach: Ensure backgrounds are visually appealing and adjusted for optimal display on mobile screens.

Image Background Removal:

  • Objective: Prepare images for development by removing existing backgrounds.
  • Design Approach: Use precise image editing techniques to isolate subjects and create transparent backgrounds.
  • Process: Edited numerous images, ensuring they were clean and ready for integration into the platform.
