Project Overview

Planlogic is a project, which involved a comprehensive redesign and enhancement of various portal functionalities. The project encompassed multiple phases, from initial client discussions to final design implementations and development handover.

Initial Client Discussion

The project kicked off with a detailed discussion with the client team to understand their company, the Statement of Advisor, and Digital RF requirements. This initial meeting was crucial to align our understanding and set clear expectations for the project deliverables.

Setting Up and Design Consistency

We began by setting up the project on a Figma instance to start the design process. To maintain design consistency, we conducted a design study based on previous design styles. Our first task was to study the existing Admin portal design and migrate it to Figma, ensuring we had a solid foundation before proceeding with updates.

Phase One: Admin Portal Updates

The initial update phase included several critical enhancements:

  • Addition of new terminals
  • Introduction of new settings and admin management features for creating and managing admins
  • Updating the Dealer group with side panels for both Advisor and Practice, allowing for the creation and update of each advisor or practice
  • Addition of a new tab for PES management
  • Enhancements in realm management, including adding and editing dealer groups
  • Intercom management for FAQs and banners
  • Comprehensive settings for admin types, profile display, and editing

Client Demonstration and Feedback

After completing the initial designs, we conducted a client demonstration to gather feedback. Based on their input, we made necessary modifications and prepared for the development phase. Due to its complexity, the PES management feature was postponed for a later stage.

Digital Presentation Portal Updates

Following the initial phase, the clients requested updates to the Digital Presentation portal. I designed the edit option for the presentation portal and, after finalizing the designs, demonstrated them to the development team. We discussed implementation details to ensure smooth development.

Further Enhancements

Under client instruction, I proceeded with additional updates to the Digital Presentation portal, focusing on:

  • Goal slide
  • Thank You slide
  • Calm PV level design

Despite the complexity of PES and Intercom management, we explored simplified design and development approaches for future implementation.

New Feature: Flowchart Design

A new client request emerged to build a flowchart feature. Following the development team's research on suitable libraries (React Flow, Diagram Maker, and React Diagrams), I designed the flowchart based on the selected library's components.

Final Updates and Demonstrations

The final tasks included redesigning the Advisor Login (New Digital SOA request) and the Presentation Upload page of the Statement of Advisor portal, incorporating a history of previously completed digital SOA requests. These designs were updated according to feedback received during demonstration sessions.

Throughout the Planlogic project, I ensured a user-centric design approach while maintaining consistency and incorporating client feedback. This project honed my skills in Figma, UI/UX design principles, and collaboration with development teams, resulting in a cohesive and functional user experience for the client.
