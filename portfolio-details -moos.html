Project Overview

I undertook this project to revamp the MOOS website, enhancing its design and functionality to align with modern web standards. The project encompassed redesigning the home page, product page, solution page, about page, and news page, ensuring the site was mobile-friendly and integrated seamlessly with a CMS.

Initial Analysis and Planning

The project began with a comprehensive analysis of the existing MOOS website. The client provided references to Lepaya and Stripe pages, which guided the design direction. My primary goal was to update the content and elements to maintain a contemporary web design while ensuring the site was structurally and conceptually robust to integrate with a CMS.

Design and Structural Upgrades

I systematically revamped the entire website, focusing on:

  • Menus and Navigation: Redesigned for better user flow and ease of access.
  • Buttons and Interactions: Updated to be more intuitive and responsive.
  • Branding: Ensured consistent branding across all pages, incorporating the company's colour scheme, typography, and visual identity.

Page-Specific Enhancements

  • Home Page: Created an engaging and dynamic entry point with key information and call-to-actions.
  • Product Page: Highlighted product features with detailed descriptions and interactive elements.
  • Solution Page: Showcased various solutions provided by MOOS, using structured layouts and clear visuals.
  • About Page: Presented the companyâ€™s mission, values, and team members through visually appealing designs.
  • News Page: Organized news articles and updates in a user-friendly manner, with a focus on readability.

Illustrations and 3D Designs

To enhance visual appeal and convey complex concepts effectively, I created several custom illustrations and 3D designs. These included:

  • Track Overtime Consumption Patterns: Visual representation to help users understand usage analytics.
  • Easy to Configure Shelves: Illustrations demonstrating the flexibility and ease of product management.
  • Product Management and Retail Shelves: Detailed 3D designs to showcase product arrangement and retail solutions.

Weekly Demonstrations and Feedback

We held weekly meetings with the client to demonstrate progress and gather feedback. These sessions were crucial for:

  • Ensuring alignment with client expectations
  • Making iterative improvements based on feedback
  • Finalizing designs before handing them over to the development team
  • Mobile-Friendly Design

Throughout the project, I prioritized making the website mobile-friendly. This involved:

  • Responsive design techniques to ensure optimal viewing on various devices
  • Testing across different screen sizes and resolutions
  • Ensuring touch-friendly interactions and layouts
