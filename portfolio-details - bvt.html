Project Overview

I led this project to revamp our company website. This project involved modernizing the site’s design, improving user experience, and adding new features to better showcase the company’s offerings and capabilities.

Initial Research and Planning

I began the project by conducting extensive research on current UI design trends and analysing similar company websites to gather inspiration. This research helped identify key areas for improvement and set the foundation for the redesign.

Identifying and Addressing Problems

In planning the redesign, I focused on addressing specific issues identified on the existing website. My goal was to implement relevant, quick, and easy design solutions to enhance the overall user experience. Key modifications included:

  • Adding a progress bar to each screen when scrolling down
  • Introducing a tech stack section to the home page
  • Modifying the footer to give it a unique look
  • Making backgrounds more attractive
  • Adjusting alignments and headers for better visual appeal
  • New Pages and Components

In addition to the modifications, the company required several new pages and components:

  • New Pages: Partnership page and What We Offer page
  • Home Page Components: Download Brochure card, carousel for the tech stack, and a contact box for home page content Design Demonstrations and Feedback

After creating the initial designs, I demonstrated them to the company and discussed potential enhancements. Based on feedback, we decided to:

  • Add a GIF to the home page for increased visual appeal
  • Introduce a contact button for quick client reach-out
  • Add two side panels of services for the What We Offer page
  • Include the About Us contact information in the footer instead of a separate page

Development Phase

Once the designs were finalized, I transitioned them from mock-ups to webpages. During this phase, I ensured consistency with the existing project and reused existing components to expedite the coding process. After completing the initial development, we held another review session with the company.

Additional Features and Updates

During the review session, we discussed adding a world map of our company clients and finalizing the footer with:

  • Download brochure option
  • Email
  • Social links
  • Company full logo
  • Alignments

After implementing these updates, the project was directed to SEO for optimization.
